What is Menopause?

Menopause is a natural phase of life when a woman’s body transitions out of its childbearing years. Think of it as a new chapter that usually begins in the late 40s or 50s.

🌞 Hot Flashes 🌙 Night Sweats 🏠 Sleep Troubles 💧 Dryness Mood Swings

Why Does Menopause Happen?

It’s simply the body’s way of saying goodbye to periods and hello to a new phase of life. It’s not a disease—it’s a natural milestone every woman goes through.

World Menopause Day

World Menopause Day is observed every year on October 18th to raise awareness about menopause and support women in this transitional phase. It’s a day to educate, empower, and encourage open conversations about the changes and challenges women experience during menopause.
